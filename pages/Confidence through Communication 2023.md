<h4>PESA 2023</h4>
<p>The YMCA Plain English Speaking Awards (PESA) 2023 is an annual competition organised by YMCA of Singapore, in partnership with the Ministry of Education and the Speak Good English Movement. The objectives of the competition are to promote public speaking in plain English as well as to provide a platform for students to hone their confidence in public presentation.</p>
