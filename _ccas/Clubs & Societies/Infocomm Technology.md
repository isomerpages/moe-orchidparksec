Members are involved in daily morning assembly duties as well as in major school events such as Sec 1 CCA Exposure, Chinese New Year Celebration, Speech Day, National Day Commemoration, Teachersâ€™ Day celebration, Graduation Day, etc. It is the duty of the club members to make sure that daily AVA support for morning flag-raising assemblies, lower or upper secondary weekly assemblies run smoothly.</p>
